WHO AM I?

Hello! I am Rafael, a passionate IT student at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela. Technology was my playground and passion since childhood. I'm actively exploring the depths of the world of IT motivated by the quest to know more about everything technology. My expedition has seen me learn Java, Figma, C, HTML, and CSS among many others.

EDUCATION

I completed my junior and senior high school education at Parada National High School. During my time in high school, I chose to take the General Academic Strand (GAS). Despite the fact that the General Academic Strand wasn’t specific to the field of technology, I remained determined to follow my passion for IT. I am now a student at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela, where I am working diligently to complete my IT degree. This step is a significant part of my journey as I aim to acquire specialized knowledge in fields like programming, system analysis, and software development.

RESERVE OFFICERS TRAINING CORPS

I am proud graduate of the Reserve Officers' Training Corps (ROTC) program. My journey in ROTC was a transformative experience that taught me the values of discipline, leadership, and patriotism. Through rigorous training and teamwork, I developed the confidence and resilience needed to face real-world challenges. Completing the program strengthened my commitment to service and shaped me into a more responsible and dependable individual.

WHAT I BRING TO THE TABLE

As an IT student, I’ve explored a variety of tools and languages that fuel my passion for technology. I began my journey with HTML and CSS, mastering how to build and design websites. Along the way, I discovered Java, a versatile language widely used for applications and software development. I also ventured into PHP and C, both essential in understanding the foundations of backend development and systems programming. Through Figma, I embraced the importance of UI/UX design in creating seamless digital experiences. Lastly, I developed a keen interest in Networking, learning how devices connect and communicate across systems, which is vital in the world of IT infrastructure. These skills form the core of my growing knowledge as I continue to explore new technologies and challenges.
